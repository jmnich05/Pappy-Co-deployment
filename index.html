CREATIVE STACK

HOW WE HANDLE IMAGES

Marketing and product development need on-brand, fast-turnaround creative without the AI-slop look. Three layers, each with a job.

ADOBE BUSINESS

Three seats. Pro tools. The fundamentals.

Adobe Business Account with Photoshop, Illustrator, and full Firefly Pro access for the creative team.

BRITTANY KELCIE KAITLYN

Why Adobe: commercially safe outputs, full editing precision, integrates with the rest of our creative stack. Photoshop + Illustrator remain the workhorses; Firefly is the AI layer for fast generation and edits.

FIREFLY × ZAPIER

The bridge between our image library and our store.

Firefly Pro integrated via Zapier to pull, edit, and push images across our stack — no manual download/upload cycle.

  • Pull source images from Google Drive or Shopify product library
  • User edits in Firefly with Image 2 or preferred LLM prompt
  • Push refined image back to Drive folder and/or Shopify product variant
  • Brand-aware presets so output matches the Pappy palette out of the gate

CLAUDE DESIGN

The new layer — concepting & product dev.

Claude's new Design feature gives us a powerful concepting tool inside the same environment we already use for everything else. Great for early-stage product, packaging, and marketing exploration.

  • Rapid concepting for packaging & product dev (Brittany lead)
  • Marketing layout exploration before committing to Adobe production
  • Brand-kit-aware via the Pappy Co Brand Kit skill
  • Stays inside the governance framework — no extra tool sprawl

WHY THIS COMBO

Answering "ChatGPT has native images, Claude doesn't."

The honest answer: native image generation isn't where Claude competes — and we don't need it to. Firefly does generative images better than ChatGPT for commercial work (rights-clear, brand-tunable) and integrates directly with our existing Adobe stack.

Claude's job is reasoning, agents, and now Design for concepting. Firefly's job is production-grade visuals. Photoshop and Illustrator are the final mile. Each tool does what it does best — no AI slop, no rights ambiguity, no workflow rework.

ANTHROPIC-FIRST.
NEVER ANTHROPIC-LOCKED.

Claude (Sonnet/Opus) is the best model for the structured, agentic work Pappy needs — voice-sensitive copy, multi-step ops workflows, customer-service triage, wholesale relationship drafting. But every artifact we build is portable:

  • Skills as markdown
  • Prompts as plain text
  • MCP for data — model-agnostic
  • Agnostic auth — SSO, not vendor-bound identity
  • RAG indices built on open formats
If Pappy ever needs to move to Gemini or OpenAI, the intelligence layer comes with you. Your investment is in your intelligence — not someone else's API.
